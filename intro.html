Introduction Talk: Exploring Visual and Musical Live Coding

Join us for an enlightening introduction to the world of live coding, where we'll delve into both visual and musical aspects of this innovative art form. Led by esteemed speakers Sol Sarratea and Staxl from Leipzig, this talk will provide fascinating insights into the creative process of live coding. Discover the magic of real-time coding as we explore its visual and auditory dimensions, and gain a deeper understanding of how live coding shapes the future of digital art and music.
