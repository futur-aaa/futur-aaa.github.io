Getting here

We recommend all visitors (especially from within Europe) to travel by train as much as possible to reduce your environmental impact.
Zwickau can be reached by train from Leipzig (line S5, S5x), from Dresden (line RE3/RB30, rail replacement service if necessary), from Chemnitz (line RB30, rail replacement service if necessary) and from Hof (line RE 3).
We recommend using the DB Navigator App and VMS website to plan your trip to Zwickau.

Accommodation

All guests with a festival ticket have the opportunity to camp at the Kunstplantage Zwickau. For all others there is the possibility to stay overnight in one of Zwickau's hotels or guesthouses. Here is a small selection:

  • Apartmenthaus am Dom - Zwickau
  • Gasthaus Alte Münze
  • Hotel Merkur
